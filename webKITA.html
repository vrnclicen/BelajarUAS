Our Heritage

Preserving History Through Exceptional Craft

At HEIRLOOM & CO., we view antiques not merely as furniture, but as tangible artifacts of human history. Each piece in our collection has been selected for its exceptional craftsmanship, unique provenance, and structural integrity.

We partner exclusively with vetted estates, private collections, and renowned auctions globally. Our restoration philosophy emphasizes preservation over perfection, maintaining the authentic patina that only centuries of careful use can provide.
